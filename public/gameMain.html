Times Up
Rules

1. Each player must fill in the a that fits in each category that starts with the given letter.

2. Playesr have until the end of the alloted time to think of words.

3. When the time is up, the words will be scored:

  1. Each valid word is worth one point
  2. Duplicate words do not count
  3. Players vote on whether or not the word fits the category

Enjoy the game!
